SPECIAL PACKAGE ACCROSS INDIA

Ayodhya

Ayodhya, steeped in mythology as the birthplace of Lord Rama, captivates visitors with its sacred sites like the Ram Janmabhoomi temple complex. Explore its historic significance through serene river ghats and immerse yourself in the city's religious fervor during vibrant festivals, offering a spiritual journey into Hindu mythology and culture.

  • More

Varanasi

Varanasi, India's spiritual nucleus, enchants with its sacred ghats, bustling markets, and mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremonies, offering a profound immersion into Hindu culture and tradition.

  • More

Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep, an archipelago of breathtaking coral islands in the Arabian Sea, invites with its pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters. Dive into the vibrant marine life, relax on secluded shores, and immerse yourself in the tranquil charm of this tropical paradise, making it an idyllic escape for nature enthusiasts and beach lovers alike.

  • More

New Delhi

New Delhi, India's capital, captivates with its rich history, from the imposing Red Fort to the serene Lotus Temple. Dive into its diverse culture through flavorful street food, bustling markets, and vibrant festivals, making it a dynamic destination for exploration and discovery.

  • More

Nanital

Nainital's tranquil lakes, framed by the majestic Himalayas, offer a serene escape for nature lovers. Embark on boat rides across Naini Lake, explore scenic hilltops, and immerse yourself in the region's natural beauty. With its charming ambiance and breathtaking vistas, Nainital promises a rejuvenating retreat in the lap of the mountains

  • More

Agra

Travel to Agra to witness the timeless allure of the Taj Mahal, a testament to eternal love. Explore the city's rich Mughal heritage through architectural wonders like the Agra Fort, and immerse yourself in its vibrant markets and culinary delights for a truly unforgettable experience.
