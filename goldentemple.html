About the Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a sacred Sikh shrine located in Amritsar, Punjab, India. It is a symbol of equality, tolerance, and spirituality.

Golden Temple Image

Special Features

The Golden Temple is known for its stunning architecture, serene environment, and the Amrit Sarovar (Pool of Nectar) which surrounds it.

Eateries Nearby

Enjoy delicious langar (free community meal) served in the Golden Temple complex. Additionally, explore local street food stalls offering Punjabi delicacies.

Estimated Expenses

For a memorable visit to the Golden Temple for a few days with two persons, expect to spend around INR 15000 including accommodation, meals, and transportation.
