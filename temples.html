Temples

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a symbol of human brotherhood and equality. The stunning architecture and the serene ambiance make it one of the most revered spiritual sites in India.

Temples

Akshardham Temple, Delhi

Akshardham Temple is a marvel of modern architecture and craftsmanship. It combines ancient Indian wisdom with modern technology to create an immersive and enlightening experience for visitors.

Temples

Meenakshi Temple, Madurai

The Meenakshi Temple is dedicated to Goddess Parvati, known as Meenakshi, and her consort Lord Shiva. It's renowned for its towering gopurams (gateway towers), intricate carvings, and vibrant festivals.

Temples

Somnath Temple, Gujarat

Somnath Temple, located on the western coast of Gujarat, is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva. It has a rich history, having been destroyed and rebuilt several times over the centuries.

Temples

Tirupati Balaji Temple, Andhra Pradesh

Tirupati Balaji Temple is one of the wealthiest and most visited temples in the world. Dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, it attracts millions of devotees each year who come to seek the blessings of the deity.

Temples

Kedarnath Temple, Uttarakhand

Kedarnath Temple, nestled in the majestic Himalayas, is one of the holiest Hindu shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva. It's a challenging pilgrimage destination that offers breathtaking views and spiritual solace.

Temples

Vaishno Devi Temple, Jammu and Kashmir

Vaishno Devi Temple, located in the Trikuta Mountains, is one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in India. Devotees trek uphill to seek the blessings of the Goddess Vaishno Devi.

Temples

Badrinath Temple, Uttarakhand

Badrinath Temple, situated on the banks of the Alaknanda River, is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites. It is dedicated to Lord Badrinath and attracts devotees from all over the world.

Temples

Jagannath Temple, Puri

Jagannath Temple is a major pilgrimage destination for Hindus, especially during the annual Rath Yatra festival. It's dedicated to Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra.
